CrimeNet Lobby Required Mod Filter

Created by Offyerrocker.
Last updated on 15-09-2019.

Download CrimeNet Lobby Required Mod Filter

If you are looking for a mod that HIDES mods which have the mods you specify, please use another mod, such as Lobby Mod Filter. This mod does essentially the opposite of that one.

This mod allows you to only show crime.net lobbies that have the mod(s) which you specify. For example, if you ONLY want to show lobbies that have Carry Stacker Reloaded or Silent Assassin, you can add these filters via the mod's options.

YOU MUST HAVE TdlQ's mod QuickKeyboardInput in order to add to or remove from the filter list!

Mod Options:

  • You can choose to either require all of the mods on your filter list, or else you can choose to require any non-zero amount (ie. at least one) of them.
  • You can choose to have the filter use case-sensitive searching.
  • You can choose to have the filter use exact matching only.
  • You can choose to allow showing unmodded lobbies in Crime.net.
  • You can add or remove mod names, or parts of a mod name, to your filter.

Again, be aware that this mod includes lobbies which have your specified mods. It does not remove lobbies for having blacklisted mods. You should install a different mod (see above) for this purpose.

Please contact me on this modpage, or on Discord or Reddit if you need help, or if you would like to report a bug. Feedback is also welcome.
