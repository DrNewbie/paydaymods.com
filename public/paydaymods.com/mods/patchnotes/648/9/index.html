12 Mar 2018 | 1.4

  • Added the ability to account for existing downs if the teammate also has this mod.
  • Added the ability to account for teammates having Nine Lives Aced if the teammate also has this mod.
  • Added ability to hide HUD via settings. While this will take effect almost instantaneously for your health circle, it may take a few seconds for your teammates' down counter HUD element to show/hide, since that element updates when they take damage.
  • Fixed down counter not resetting downs for teammates who are released from custody (in games where you are not the host).
  • Fixed a potential crash on releasing teammates from custody in games that you are hosting.
  • Minor, nearly-insignificant text fix from v1.30
