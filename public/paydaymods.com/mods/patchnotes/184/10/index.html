Changelog 1.22

Class-wide Changes

Weapons

  • Shotgun damage falloff reworked
  • Raven/Steakout 12G/IZHMA and all secondary shotguns: 8-25m, 0%
  • Steakout 12G/M1014: 11-30m, 0%
  • Mosconi/Joceline: 13-35m, 0%

Weapon Mods

  • Slug Accuracy increased from 8 to 20

Specific Weapon Changes

  • M308 Accuracy reduced by 4

Bug Fixes

  • Shotgun Slugs now penetrate more reliably through shields
  • Shotgun Slugs now have their intended 50% falloff minimum damage instead of 0% falloff minimum damage.

Back-end

  • YOMCMods has been rewritten to have better compatibility with other mods.
  • Script disabling skins from giving stat boosts has been disabled.
  • Older legacy code has been removed.
